Sunday School
             Sunday school is a Christian educational institution for children and adults. Through teaching, learning, and companionship, the objective of Sunday School is to teach the gospel of Jesus Christ and to develop individuals and families. Thanks to God's grace and the efforts of all the principals, vice-principals, teachers, parents, and especially amazing kids who have engaged in our ministry over the years, we have a superb Sunday School here at SBGOC.
